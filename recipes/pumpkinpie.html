Pumpkin Pie

Description

Pumpkin Pie is a fall favorite with a smooth, spiced pumpkin custard filling baked in a flaky pie crust. Warm, earthy spices like cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger bring out the rich flavor of pumpkin, making it a staple on Thanksgiving tables and cozy autumn evenings.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) unbaked pie crust
  • 1 can (15 oz) pure pumpkin puree
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • Whipped cream (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix sugar, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
  3. Add pumpkin and eggs; beat until well combined.
  4. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
  5. Pour the mixture into the unbaked pie crust.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F.
  7. Reduce temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for an additional 40–50 minutes, or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool on a wire rack for at least 2 hours.
  9. Serve with whipped cream if desired. Refrigerate leftovers.
