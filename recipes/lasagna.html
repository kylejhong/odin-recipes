Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian-American classic that layers tender pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey melted cheese. Perfect for gatherings or a comforting family dinner, this oven-baked dish is beloved for its savory depth and satisfying texture. Whether served fresh or as leftovers, lasagna only gets better with time.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage (optional)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add ground beef and sausage, cooking until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, a pinch of salt, and pepper.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat for three layers.
  9. Finish with meat sauce and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  11. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
